Stimuli Base

JURICS Base: Joint Universal Real-world Images with the Continuous States, developed with Natalia Tiurina, Nikita Michalev, and Igor Utochkin.

  • 98 categories of objects
  • 2 exemplars per category
  • 20 states per exemplar
  • Validation with 56 observers
Current source page

Validation Process

  • Observers named exemplars
  • Rated familiarity and visual complexity
  • Identified categories and dynamic states
  • Named final states and rated familiarity of changing states
  • Produced preliminary correlation matrices and category means

Research Notes

The final image set is planned to include 3920 JPG or PNG images and 196 GIFs. The original page notes that the database is still under validation.
